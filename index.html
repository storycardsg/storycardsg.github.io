What’s your story?

We do not remember days, we remember moments.
Cesare Pavese

What’s your story? Yes, you. Is it inside that box of random photos that you never get to organize? In the endless scroll of photostream? Or is it inside that whole desk drawer of old, orphaned photos that your family has been passing down from one generation to the next?

Each moment of your life holds a story. The wonder, the fear, and the sheer joy – all of these emotions drive your every action and weave your stories. Each story gives you an insight into yourselves and others – a meaningful lesson.

Time passes – days into months, months into years. In the blink of an eye, a moment passes. We can help you catch these fleeting moments. We help you find the magic in those moments that really inspire you and save these stories in a beautiful legacy to share with your family.

How does our storycard look like?

We design our storycard to enable an inspiring reading/visual experience. Each storycard will feature your selected photo printed on one side with the usual look and feel of photographs and the accompanying story on the back of the card. The 4R double-sided card (4 x 6") makes it easy for you to keep, enjoy and share your stories with your family and friends.
